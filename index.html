Nexus is a unique ocean world where chemosynthesis thrives. Unlike Earth's photosynthesis-based ecosystems, Nexus's life forms derive energy from chemical reactions in the absence of sunlight.

This alien environment hosts a diverse array of organisms adapted to extreme conditions, offering valuable insights into the potential for life in the cosmos.
